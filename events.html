    Our Policy

    The New Leaf Team Room offers a variety of musical, educational & theatrical presentations throughout the year. All of our special events are prepaid reservations only and are non-refundable. There will be No credits issued in the event that you cancel your pre-paid ticketed reservation for any reason. Your food will be packaged for you or a designated person to pick up at the time of ticketed event. You are not permitted to reschedule your pre-paid ticketed event under no circumstances. We thank you for your understanding .
