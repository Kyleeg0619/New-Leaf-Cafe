    We are conveniently located at 606 Main Street in Riverton, NJ. Our private parking lot is located behind the New Leaf driveway, accessible from Main Street.

    The New Leaf Tea Room & Gift Shoppe is open Thursday through Saturday 11:00am- 4:00pm. Open on Sundays for Private Parties and Brunch 10:00 a.m. till 1:00p.m

    Afternoon Tea is by reservation Only, A valid credit card is required at time of booking to confirm your reservation. Our first Seating is at 12:00 noon Thursdays and Fridays; and Saturdays between 12:00 p.m. and 2:00 p.m. We do not serve Afternoon Tea on Sundays. You may make a request for which room you would prefer to be seated in but we are unable to guarantee this request. Porch seating may be requested but is not guaranteed.

    Reservations for Brunch are highly recommended but not required, Brunch is available on Select Sundays between 10:00 a.m. and 1:00 p.m. Indoor and Outdoor seating (weather permitting)

    Our cancellation policy is as follows:

    In order to be courteous to all guests trying to make a reservation at one of our tea seatings in our limited dinning rooms, we ask that you call within 24 hours of your reservation time to make any necessary changes or cancellations. If at the time of your scheduled seating there are less guests than the original reservation without notice of the changes, or if the reservation is not cancelled prior to 24 hours in advance, your credit card will be charged for the original amount of the number of guests on the original reservation.
